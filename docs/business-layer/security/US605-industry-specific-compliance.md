## Objective
Ensure that the system adheres to all industry-specific compliance requirements, providing users with confidence that their operations meet legal and regulatory standards.

## Description
As a user, I want the system to comply with industry-specific regulations, ensuring that the system meets all legal and regulatory standards relevant to my industry.

## Requirements
- **CR002**: Legal - System should comply with industry-specific regulations and standards.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **Given** the system is processing user data **when** the data is stored **then** it must comply with GDPR regulations.
- [ ] **Given** the system is handling sensitive data **when** data anonymization is required **then** it must use techniques compliant with ISO/IEC 20889:2018 standards.
- [ ] **Given** the system is generating reports **when** the reports are created **then** they must include all necessary compliance-related information.
- [ ] **Given** the system is updated **when** new compliance regulations are introduced **then** it must be updated to reflect these changes within 30 days.
- [ ] **Given** the system is accessed by users **when** they log in **then** it must verify their compliance-related permissions.

## Tasks
1. **Research Compliance Requirements**
    - Identify all relevant industry-specific regulations and standards.
    - Consult with legal and compliance experts to validate requirements.

2. **Design Compliance Features**
    - Design data storage mechanisms to ensure GDPR compliance.
    - Develop data anonymization techniques compliant with ISO/IEC 20889:2018.
    - Plan the structure and content of compliance-related reports.

3. **Implement Compliance Mechanisms**
    - Develop and integrate features to ensure data storage complies with GDPR.
    - Implement data anonymization techniques as per ISO/IEC 20889:2018.
    - Create report generation functionality to include compliance-related information.

4. **Update System for New Regulations**
    - Monitor for new compliance regulations.
    - Update the system to reflect new regulations within 30 days of their introduction.

5. **User Access and Permissions**
    - Implement user login verification to check compliance-related permissions.

6. **Testing and Validation**
    - Conduct thorough testing to ensure all compliance features work as intended.
    - Perform regular audits to verify ongoing compliance.

7. **Documentation and Training**
    - Document all compliance features and updates.
    - Train team members on compliance requirements and their roles.

8. **Feedback and Improvement**
    - Collect user feedback on compliance-related features.
    - Make necessary improvements based on feedback and audit results.

## Notes
- Regular audits and reviews should be conducted to ensure ongoing compliance with industry regulations.
- Consider using automated tools to monitor compliance and generate reports.
- Ensure that all team members are aware of the compliance requirements and their roles in meeting them.
- Keep documentation up-to-date with any changes in compliance requirements or system updates.
- Engage with legal and compliance experts to validate the system's adherence to regulations.
- User feedback should be collected to identify any compliance-related issues or concerns.
