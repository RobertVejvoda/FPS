title: Billing
---

[Billing](../technology-layer/billing) is responsible for handling all financial transactions within the system. It ensures secure processing of payments, manages billing, and integrates with various payment gateways.

### Application Functions (Archimate Perspective)

- **Payment Method Management**: Provision and configuration of various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets.

- **Payment Gateway Integration**: Seamless integration with multiple payment gateways to ensure processing flexibility and reliability.

- **Billing Automation**: Automated generation of invoices and billing cycles to reduce manual intervention and minimize errors.

- **Invoice Customization**: Enables tenants to tailor invoice templates and branding to meet their specific requirements.

- **Real-time Financial Reporting**: Offers dynamic dashboards and analytics for continuous monitoring of transaction flows and financial metrics.

- **Fraud Detection and Prevention**: Implements advanced controls to identify and prevent fraudulent transactions.

- **Discount and Promotion Management**: Provides tools for creating, managing, and automatically applying discounts and promotional campaigns.

- **Invoice Generation**: Automatically produces detailed invoices, including line items for all charges at the end of each billing cycle.

- **Notification Management**: Sends automated notifications for upcoming payments, failed transactions, and overdue invoices.

- **User Account Management**: Allows users to update payment details and access a complete history of invoices and payments.

- **Refund and Dispute Management**: Streamlines the processing of refunds and enables users to dispute charges through a structured process.

- **Subscription Management**: Supports various subscription models, including monthly, annual, pay-as-you-go, and freemium approaches.

- **Refund Processing**: Efficiently handles refund requests to ensure they are processed within specified timeframes.

- **Transaction History Management**: Maintains a comprehensive archive of all transactions for a minimum of five years.

- **Dispute Resolution Management**: Provides a documented mechanism for disputing transactions and resolving issues.
