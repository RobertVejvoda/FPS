We understand the importance of affordability. We offer a special rate to ensure that the cost is manageable, even for large enterprises that typically manage their processes in Excel and may not have extra budget.

- **Small Businesses**: $0.50 per employee per month (for companies with fewer than 50 employees)
- **Medium Businesses**: $1 per employee per month (for companies with 50 to 250 employees)
- **Large Businesses**: $2 per employee per month (for companies with more than 250 employees)

This subscription model ensures that all businesses, regardless of size, can benefit from our services without straining their budgets. Additionally, we offer a payback program where companies can earn credits towards their subscription by referring other businesses to our service.

For more information or to discuss custom licensing options, please contact our sales team at [sales@redarc.com](mailto:sales@redarc.com).
